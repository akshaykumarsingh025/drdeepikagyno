About Your Doctor

Meet Dr. Deepika

Dedicated to empowering women through compassionate healthcare and expert medical guidance.

Experience You Can Trust

Dr. Deepika Singh is a renowned Gynecologist and Obstetrician with over 10 years of experience at prestigious institutions including AIIMS New Delhi, Max Super Speciality Hospital, and Apollo Cradle.

As an MD (Gynecology and Obstetrics) from AIIMS New Delhi (2012-2015), she specializes in managing high-risk pregnancies, advanced laparoscopic and hysteroscopic surgeries, infertility treatments, and comprehensive women's healthcare from adolescence to menopause.

Her philosophy is rooted in listening to her patients. She believes that every woman deserves to be heard and understood. Her clinic provides a safe, non-judgmental environment where patients can discuss their most intimate health concerns with confidence.
